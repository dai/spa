Preloading is based on a stack (LIFO) structure. The most relevant, newest paths are pushed onto the stack, and taken to be preloaded on interval basis.

The delay for poping and preloading items from stack has a default value, but it can be altered per controller by defining the property options.preloadStackDelay. The limitation is that if different controllers have different delays set, the new delay will not take effect until the stack is emptied.

The preload stack is of limited size, so browsing around would not create too many preloading requests of low relevance.
